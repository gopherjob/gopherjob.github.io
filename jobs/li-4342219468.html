Senior Software Engineer (Go, React & AWS)

Industry: Digital | SaaS | Compliance

Location: Manchester / Hybrid (2 - 3 days a week in the office)

Salary: £70,000 – £90,000

Are you a seasoned Senior Engineer with strong expertise in Go and AWS, but also happy to lead on front-end React work, and ready to take on a pivotal role in building high-performing platforms? This is an opportunity to join an ambitious, scaling tech business that’s delivering cutting-edge solutions to a global market.

The Opportunity

You’ll be joining a team working on the development of an industry-leading product right from the ground up. This role blends hands-on coding, R&D of new technologies, and solving complex technical problems, while collaborating across disciplines to deliver secure, scalable, and reliable software.

What You’ll Do

  • Lead by example through coding, design, and problem-solving, setting the bar for technical excellence.
  • Take ownership across the stack: frontend (React/Remix), backend (Go), and AWS infrastructure.
  • Contribute to technical design and bring fresh ideas to the table.
  • Drive automation and scripting across MacOS and Linux systems.
  • Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines using GitHub Actions and Infrastructure as Code principles.
  • Mentor colleagues, supporting their growth through reviews, pair programming, and open collaboration.
  • Partner with product and design teams to align technical solutions with business goals.
  • Ensure security best practices are embedded throughout.

What You’ll Bring

  • A generalist mindset, comfortable moving across technologies and frameworks.
  • Strong AWS and Go experience
  • Experience working with large front-end applications (React)
  • React Native experience is a big plus but not essential
  • A passion for problem-solving and tackling challenges that don’t have straightforward answers.
  • Excitement for working in an early-stage product environment where you can help shape architecture and ways of working.
  • Adaptability, ownership, and a collaborative nature.

What’s on Offer

  • Salary: 70k - 90k
  • 25 days’ holiday + bank holidays (plus your birthday off)
  • Flexible working hours and locations, with hybrid/remote options.
  • Juno for health / wellbeing (£100 per month)
  • Vitality health insurance + cash plan
  • Royal London pension plan
  • Life Insurance
  • Regular social events and activities organised by a dedicated committee.
  • MacBook Pro provided.
  • Competitive pension (increasing contribution planned).
