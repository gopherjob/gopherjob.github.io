Responsibilities Business Development

  • Identify, approach, and acquire new clients in the IT industry, particularly in the Japanese market.

Client Relationship Management

  • Act as an Account Manager, building long-term relationships with clients to understand their needs and provide tailored solutions.
  • Maintain regular communication with existing clients and ensure customer satisfaction.

Sales Execution

  • Manage the entire sales cycle, including negotiation, proposal preparation, and closing deals.
  • Develop and present business proposals, solutions, and case studies for prospective clients.

Collaboration

  • Work closely with the sales and technical teams in Vietnam to provide seamless solutions to clients.

Reporting

  • Prepare sales forecasts, reports, and performance analysis for management.

Requirements

  • International Sales
  • Japanese
  • English

Must-Have

  • Minimum 6 months of experience in IT Sales for the Japanese market.
  • Japanese language proficiency (JLPT N2 or higher).
  • Strong communication, negotiation, and presentation skills.
  • Proven ability to identify, approach, and convert leads into long-term clients.

Preferred

  • Experience in International Sales or Account Management roles.
  • Proficiency in English is a plus.
  • Attractive appearance is considered a bonus.

Benefits

  • Salary range: Up to 400 man/year + commission
  • Round-trip flight support to your home country (once per year)
  • House rent subsidy
  • Performance Review: 1 time/year
  • Young and dynamic working environment.
  • Work closely with seniors and managers to gain hands-on experience and accelerate professional growth.
  • Opportunity to approach the latest technology trends
  • Extra Medical Insurance and Annual health check-ups.
  • Other benefits: Summer vacation, birthday, team building… are held periodically.
