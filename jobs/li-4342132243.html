Company Description

ReveniQ transforms how businesses grow their SaaS software revenue through a cutting-edge cloud-based data analytics platform. Our platform provides advanced tools for managing invoices, processing subscriptions with pro-rata charges, and analyzing key revenue metrics such as MRR, ARR, CLTV, and churn rates. We prioritize customer retention and growth, offering businesses insights to identify and maximize opportunities. ReveniQ seamlessly integrates with platforms like Stripe, Salesforce, Xero, and Netsuite, creating a centralized financial data hub for efficient revenue management.

Role Description

This is a full-time remote role for a Senior Full Stack Rails/GO Developer. The responsibilities include designing and developing scalable web applications, building robust back-end services, and collaborating on the development of front-end UI/UX features. You will work with a team of developers, designers, and stakeholders on the evolution of our data analytics platform, implementing high-quality, maintainable software solutions to deliver exceptional user experiences.

Qualifications

  • Expertise in Back-End Web Development, with strong skills in Rails and GO programming.
  • Proficiency in Front-End Development, including experience with modern frameworks and technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Demonstrated experience in Full-Stack Development and Software Development for scalable web applications.
  • Experience designing and implementing responsive, user-friendly interfaces using technologies like Cascading Style Sheets (CSS).
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a remote environment, with strong problem-solving and critical-thinking skills.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Experience with SaaS platforms, cloud-based solutions, and tools such as Stripe, Salesforce, Xero, and/or Netsuite is a strong plus.
